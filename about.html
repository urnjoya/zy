About Toolzy

Professional online tools for everyone. Fast, free, and privacy-focused.

Our Mission

Toolzy was created with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, accessible online tools that anyone can use without registration, downloads, or hidden fees.

We believe productivity tools should be fast, reliable, and respect your privacy. That's why all our tools run entirely in your browser‚Äîyour data never leaves your device.

What We Offer

Toolzy provides a comprehensive collection of online utilities across multiple categories:

  • Text & Writing Tools: Word counters, case converters, text generators, and more for writers and content creators.
  • Developer Tools: JSON formatters, code minifiers, encoding/decoding utilities for programmers.
  • Security & Privacy Tools: Password generators, hash generators, and encryption tools to keep your data secure.
  • Random & Utility Tools: UUID generators, QR code creators, and various practical utilities.

Privacy First

Your privacy is our top priority. All tools operate 100% client-side using JavaScript in your web browser. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your data to our servers.

No cookies for tracking, no analytics that invade your privacy, and no accounts required. Just pure, functional tools that respect your data.

Always Free

All our tools are completely free to use with no hidden fees, premium tiers, or paywalls. We believe essential productivity tools should be accessible to everyone.

We may display non-intrusive advertisements to support the platform, but all core functionality remains free forever.
