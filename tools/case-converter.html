Frequently Asked Questions

Title case capitalizes the first letter of each word, commonly used for headings and titles.
camelCase is a naming convention where the first letter is lowercase and each subsequent word starts with a capital letter, with no spaces.
snake_case uses underscores to separate words (common in Python), while kebab-case uses hyphens (common in URLs and CSS).
